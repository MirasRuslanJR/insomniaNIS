The problem

Climate change, resource mismanagement, and urban growth create complex environmental challenges.

People lack interactive tools to understand how everyday actions influence real ecological systems. There's no trust in claimed eco-actions, no motivation to continue, and no visible connection between personal choices and global climate impact.

Solution - What is EcoChain

EcoChain is a platform combining real-world eco actions with a climate simulation system. Users complete local environmental tasks, verify each other's impact, and see how collective decisions influence sustainability.

Live EcoMap

Interactive map with real-time updates showing ecological problems and solutions in your city

Photo-Proof System

Document your actions with before/after photos, timestamps, and geolocation for verification

Peer Verification

Community validates actions through peer review. 3 confirmations = verified eco-action

Carbon Dashboard

Personal dashboard visualizing your carbon footprint with real-time CO₂ calculations

District Challenges

Local challenges for your district with collective goals and community rewards

Rewards Hub

Exchange EcoPoints for real rewards from local eco-businesses and partners

Measurable Climate Impact

Every action converts to kilograms of saved CO₂. See the real impact of your choices on the climate.

Cleanup = 2 kg CO₂

Prevents emissions from waste decomposition

Tree Planting = 22 kg CO₂/year

One tree absorbs CO₂ throughout its lifetime

Climate Survival: Base Under Threat

A strategic survival game where your decisions shape the climate and determine your base's future

Game Concept

You're a researcher managing a limited base. The world suffers periodic climate events: heatwaves, floods, droughts. Collect resources, build ecological solutions, and protect your base from disasters.

Exploration

Explore the map, gather resources: water, metal, biomass

Building

Construct shelter, water collectors, solar panels, plant trees

Consequences

Your decisions affect the frequency and intensity of climate events

Victory

Reduce carbon_level below threshold to stabilize the climate
